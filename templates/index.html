About Me

Introduction

I am a Junior Data Analyst with hands-on experience in budget forecasting and financial planning, working on enterprise-level projects for Hindustan Unilever Limited (HUL) at Algorisys Technologies Pvt. Ltd. I specialize in IBM Planning Analytics (TM1), Cognos Analytics, Power BI, and Excel, supporting monthly and annual forecast cycles through accurate data validation and reporting.

My role involves collaborating closely with business users to understand requirements and translate them into effective data models, dashboards, and reporting solutions. I have strong experience in forecast input validation, cube reconciliation, system releases, and production support, ensuring reliable and timely business insights.

In addition to planning and BI tools, I leverage Python, SQL, and analytical libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to automate data processing and build reusable, self-service reporting solutions. I enjoy transforming complex datasets into clear insights that support strategic decision-making.

I am continuously learning and improving my skills, with a strong interest in data analytics, financial planning, and business intelligence, and I aim to deliver accurate, scalable, and user-focused data solutions.
